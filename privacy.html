Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026  ·  801 Visual LLC  ·  Salt Lake City, Utah

801 Visual LLC ("801 Visual," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at www.801visual.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, phone number, and any message content you provide.
  • Scheduling via Calendly: when you book a call through our Calendly link, Calendly collects your name, email, and scheduling information. This data is subject to Calendly's Privacy Policy.

We do not currently use cookies for tracking or analytics beyond what is technically necessary to serve the website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or quote request.
  • Schedule and conduct discovery calls.
  • Communicate about projects, services, and pricing.
  • Fulfill any services you engage us for.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Communications

By submitting our contact form, you consent to being contacted by 801 Visual via email, phone, or SMS in connection with your inquiry. Standard message and data rates may apply to SMS communications. You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP.

4. Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or as required by applicable law. If you would like us to delete your information, contact us at admin@801visual.com.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may link to or integrate third-party services (such as Calendly for scheduling and Instagram for social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect when it was last revised. Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us
